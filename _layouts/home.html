Capabilities

For clients that already have a project in mind, here are examples of Artificial Intelligence solutions that Neural Swarm can build.

Anomaly Detection

Finding outliers that are different from most of the data.
E.g. in a database of financial transactions, outliers may be fraudulent transactions.

Chatbots

Text and voice conversations using an automated dialogue system.
E.g. 24/7 customer service addressing a broad range of issues in natural language.

Content Moderation

Detecting inappropriate content within images, video and text.
E.g. prevent toxicity in online communities by pro-actively moderating user-generated content.

Object Classification

Detecting and labeling instances of objects in images and video.
E.g. identifying defective products on a manufacturing production line using images.

Recommender Systems

Producing recommendations that target individual customers.
E.g. identifying recommended products based on preferences of other customers with similar attributes.

Sentiment Analysis

Determining whether a view on a subject is positive, negative or neutral.
E.g. monitoring customer brand perception on social media.

Time Series Forecasting

Predicting a numerical quantity in the future given historical data points.
E.g. predicting next month’s grocery store sales.

Translation

Real-time audio and text translation between languages.
E.g. offer instant translations of user-generated content to reach a global audience.

Recent Work

Explore some of our recent projects where we’ve made an outsized impact.

Horse Race Betting Model

Collaborated with a Hong Kong betting syndicate to model the win probability of horses in races at Happy Valley and Sha Tin by using Maximum Likelihood Estimation (MLE) to adjust parameters. These win probabilities were extended beyond win bets to place, quinella and quinella place bets.

Package Delivery Time Simulation

A simulator was built for an Australian delivery company to assess business model viability. The simulator modelled the distribution of package delivery times in urban areas using uniformly generated point-to-point ETAs and run over different times and days to account for seasonality. The project was deployed as an interactive tool comprising of a delivery time Isochrone overlaid over an urban map.

Increasing License Plate Recognition Accuracy

Helped an Australian vendor increase the accuracy of an existing license plate recognition (LPR) system by merging alternate sources of data. The extra sensor data required real-time complex event processing (CEP) and unsupervised learning to extract a clean data stream in order to augment the current system.

Worker Productivity and Wellness

Developed and deployed a series of business intelligence dashboards that monitor factory worker productivity and wellness for a Hong Kong startup with a focus on Corporate Social Responsbility (CSR). A series of reports, metrics and data visualisations were also researched and designed.
